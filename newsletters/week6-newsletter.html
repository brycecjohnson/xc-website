üèÉ HEALTH TIP OF THE WEEK

Recovery Between Multiple Races

Racing three times in one week requires smart recovery strategies. Immediately after each race, start your recovery with a proper cool-down jog of at least 10-15 minutes, even if you're tired. This helps flush metabolic waste from your muscles.

Between races, prioritize these recovery tools: 1) Hydration - aim for half your body weight in ounces of water daily, 2) Sleep - add an extra 30-60 minutes if possible, 3) Nutrition - eat protein within 30 minutes post-race (chocolate milk works great!), 4) Gentle movement - easy walking or cycling helps recovery more than complete rest. Consider foam rolling or gentle stretching, but avoid aggressive massage between races.
