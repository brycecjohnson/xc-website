üèÉ HEALTH TIP OF THE WEEK

State Meet Week Preparation

Sleep (State Athletes): Aim for 9+ hours every night this week. Tuesday and Wednesday nights are especially critical - the sleep you get 2-3 nights before the race matters more than Friday night sleep.

Hydration: Consistent water intake throughout the week. Pale yellow urine indicates proper hydration. Don't try to "catch up" on hydration Friday night - start early in the week.

Nutrition: Maintain your normal, healthy diet Monday-Thursday. Friday, slightly increase carbohydrates at meals (extra rice, pasta, or sweet potatoes). Saturday breakfast should be familiar foods eaten 3-4 hours before your race - no experiments on race day!

Mental Preparation: Visualize the State Meet course and your race plan. See yourself running strong, handling tough moments, and finishing with everything you have. State Meet nerves are normal - channel that energy into your performance.

Rest and Recovery: Limit intense physical activity outside of practice. Avoid standing for long periods. Elevate your legs when watching TV. Let your body store energy for Saturday.
