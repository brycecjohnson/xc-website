üìå IMPORTANT REMINDERS

Workout Execution:

  • Always complete a proper warm-up (10-15 min easy jog + dynamic stretches)
  • Cool-down after every workout (10 min easy jog + static stretching)
  • Adjust paces based on weather conditions (slow down 10-15 sec/mile on hot days)
  • Listen to your body - communicate any pain or excessive fatigue to coaches

Recovery & Nutrition:

  • Aim for 8+ hours of sleep every night
  • Hydrate throughout the day (minimum half your body weight in ounces)
  • Eat within 30 minutes after hard workouts and races
  • Ice baths or cold water immersion after hard efforts

Race Week Protocol:

  • Reduce training volume by 30-40%
  • Maintain intensity but reduce repetitions
  • Extra focus on sleep and nutrition
  • Visualize race strategy and stay positive

Communication:

  • Report any illness or injury immediately
  • Ask questions if workout instructions are unclear
  • Support your teammates - we win as a team!
